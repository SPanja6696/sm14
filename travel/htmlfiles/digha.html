Digha

Digha is a popular seaside resort town in the state of West Bengal. It's located on the coast of the Bay of Bengal, about 187 km southwest of Kolkata. The town is known for its untouched beaches and scenic views. The town is popular destination for familes looking to spend a pleasant weekend. It's also popular among short trip seekers. Digha is also known as the "Brighton of the East".
